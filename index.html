About the game

⚔️Makk Attakk: Attack of the Makk⚔️ is a 2D platformer game where you play as Makk the duck🐤



❗Makk the duck is a highly dangerous criminal❗


Makk has faced prison his entire life for crimes including but not limited to:


    Arson 🔥
    Assault 🤛
    Bank robbery 🏦💰
    Battery 🔋
    Breaking and entering 🏠🔨
    Cannibalism
    Conspiracy 👁️
    Conspiracy of mass destruction of the alligator species 💣 🐊
    Domestic violence 🏠 🤛
    Dismemberment of other animals
    Identity fraud 🚫
    Insurance fraud 📝🚫
    Motor vehicle theft 🚗 🏃
    Theft 🏃 🏬
    Tax evasion 🏃 📝
    Treason ⛔
    Vandalism 🧱 🖌️
    Wire fraud 💳 🚫

But Makk has a secret; he he has a past with alligators, and the most ferocious one has chased him into the sewers of deep Florida! Can you escape Darth Gader?🐊
